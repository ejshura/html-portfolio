The Best Movies of All Time According to Ethan

My top 3 movies of all time.

Nerve (2016)

A story about an anonymous online game of Truth or Dare, without the Truth. This movie has a great soundtrack and makes incredible use of lighting and camera work to further add to suspensful moments.

Nick and Norah's Infinite Playlist (2008)

A journey through New York's indie rock scene creates a fate encounter for two teens. I love the soundtrack and the characters are super funny and relatable.

Project X (2012)

The story of a high school kid who sets out to throw the greatest party of all time. Great music, incredible camera work, and the movie really takes you along for the ride.
